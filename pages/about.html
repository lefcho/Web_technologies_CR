About Me

Hello! I’m Pesho, the creative eye behind PhotoMoto. Ever since I picked up my first camera at age 12, I’ve been passionate about capturing the stories that unfold in everyday moments.

Over the past 5 years, I’ve specialized in:

  • Portrait photography—bringing out genuine emotion
  • Landscape photography—showcasing the beauty of nature
  • Event photography—preserving those once-in-a-lifetime memories

My philosophy is simple: tell a story through light. Whether it’s the warm glow of sunset on a couple’s faces or the dramatic contrast of a city skyline at night, I aim to freeze those moments so they live on forever.
